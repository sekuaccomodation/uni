Welcome to Seku University Hostel Accommodation

Seku University provides comfortable hostel accommodation for its students. To secure a room, please make a payment of 4000 Kenyan Shillings using M-Pesa.

Accommodation Details

Our students are accommodated in the Main Campus. Both government-sponsored and self-sponsored students are accommodated on a first come first served basis upon clearance of tuition and accommodation fees. Currently, SEKU has the following hostels:

  • Victoria
  • Turkwell
  • Elementaita
  • Athi
  • Baringo
  • Yatta
  • Magadi
  • Turkana

Some of the hostels have attached kitchens to serve the residents. Kitchen One serves the rest of the hostels. The students are served well-prepared quality meals at affordable prices in the kitchens.

The Directorate of Catering and Accommodation Services falls under the Division of Finance, Planning, and Development and is headed by the Director, who, along with her team of well-qualified staff, undertakes its operations.

At the Directorate of Catering and Accommodation:

  • We believe that the students are our number one customers.
  • We are ready to respond to our customer needs.
  • We retain a high level of transparency and accountability.

Hostel Allocation

Hostel allocation at Seku University is done by the school administration. The allocation process considers various factors such as student's year of study, availability of rooms, and any specific requirements mentioned during the application process.

The purpose of this approach is to ensure fair and efficient allocation of hostels to the students. The school administration strives to provide a conducive living environment for all the students, taking into account their preferences and needs.

Please note that hostel allocation is not under the control of individual students. The school administration will communicate the allocated hostel to each student before the start of the academic year.

Payment Details

To make the payment, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your M-Pesa menu
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"
  3. Select "Pay Bill"
  4. Enter the Business Number: 247247
  5. Enter the Account Number: sekuaccommodation
  6. Enter the amount: 4000
  7. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and confirm the transaction
  8. Save the confirmation message for future reference
